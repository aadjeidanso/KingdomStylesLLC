WHO WE ARE

Kingdom Styles LLC is a professional and natural hair braiding destination that focuses on providing outstanding customer care & high-quality natural hair styles. Each hair style is crafted with the utmost care and passion to exceed your expectations.

Our customers not only love their hair, but also our clean and modern place, easy to use online booking, and the warm atmosphere around.

Our Values

Online Booking

Easily book online anytime and anywhere. Calling and texting is optional.

No walk-ins

For a better experience, we take appointments only. No walk-ins are accepted.

Clean and Safe

Your safety is top of mind. We follow strict sanitation guidelines.

No Wait time

Because you book online, there's very little to no wait time when you show on-time for your appointment.

Transparency

Our pricing is transparent and available upfront when booking. No haggling necessary.

Fun

You might catch us dancing to afro-beats or singing Beyoncé. We love our work and it shows daily.

Why choose Kingdom Styles?

  • Perfect Braids. Everytime.

    We love what we do, we take the time we need to listen to your needs and give you perfect, neat, and well-defined braids every time.

  • Your health and safety first.

    We follow strict sanitation and safety protocols. Each station is sanitized after use and all of our equipment is sterilized.

  • We take pride in our work.

    We take great pride in our work and put a lot of effort when serving you. Braiding requires patience from both customers and our staff, we ensure that you are comfortable and satisfied with our work.
